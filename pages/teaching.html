Physics GRE Subject Test Prep

For the past three years, I have organized and taught a short preparatory course on the Physics GRE Subject Test. The course is intended for third- or fourth-year undergraduates who are interested in applying for graduate school in physics, and includes a review of topics from undergraduate physics as well as time-saving strategies for approaching problems on the test.
